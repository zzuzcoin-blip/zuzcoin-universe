About Us

ZUZ Universe is a philosophical and economic experiment on the Ethereum blockchain. It introduces the concept of "Time as Currency" — a digital asset where value is measured not by speculation, but by patience, belief, and long-term commitment.

The project is named after the ancient Talmudic coin "Zuz" (pl. Zuzim), a symbol of fair trade, transparency, and tangible value. ZUZ revives this spirit in the digital age.
